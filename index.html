Join the Guild

The Seattle Printers Guild is a group of artists that organize fun, free printmaking events available to all. We host monthly workshops around Seattle and King County focused on print and art-making, covering topics like block printing, screen printing, bookbinding, and zine making. No experience necessary, all ages welcome!
